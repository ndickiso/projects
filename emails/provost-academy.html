Don't Let It Pass You By
 
The days go slow but the years go fast. At Provost Academy we help you navigate both as you press on toward your goal of graduation.

When you enroll with us, one of the things we send to you is your tassel. It is the symbol that represents the commitment you have made to yourself, and the commitment we have made to you. It also serves as a visual reminder that the goal is for you to walk across the stage in honor of the hard work and dedication you have given to yourself.

However, it is not the finish line. Instead, we like to think of graduation as the launching pad. It is the platform to let the future know you are on your way.
